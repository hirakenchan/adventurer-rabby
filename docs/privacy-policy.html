Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile game Adventurer Rabby.

1. Overview

Adventurer Rabby is a casual dungeon puzzle game developed and published by hiraken.

This game is designed to be played without creating an account. The app does not require users to enter personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or address.

2. Information We Collect

Adventurer Rabby itself does not directly collect personal information from users.

The game may store gameplay data locally on the user’s device, such as:

  • highest stage reached
  • number of steps
  • ranking records
  • game progress-related values

This information is stored only on the device and is not sent to our server.

3. Advertising

Adventurer Rabby uses Google AdMob to display rewarded advertisements.

When users choose to watch an advertisement, Google AdMob may collect and process certain information, such as device information, advertising identifiers, approximate location, app usage information, and ad interaction data, for purposes such as ad delivery, ad measurement, fraud prevention, and service improvement.

For more information about how Google handles data, please refer to Google’s privacy policies and AdMob-related information.

4. Rewarded Ads

The game includes rewarded ads that users may choose to watch voluntarily.

For example, users may watch an advertisement to continue playing after a game over. Watching rewarded ads is optional. Users can continue to use the game without watching ads, although some optional in-game benefits may not be available.

5. Children’s Privacy

Adventurer Rabby is intended for a general audience and is not specifically directed to children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through this app, please contact us so that appropriate action can be taken.

6. Third-Party Services

This app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify users.

Third-party service used by this app:

  • Google AdMob

Please refer to the privacy policies of these third-party services for more information about how they collect and use data.

7. Data Sharing

Adventurer Rabby does not sell users’ personal information.

The app does not directly share personal information with third parties. However, third-party services such as Google AdMob may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information handled by the app. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is completely secure.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Users are encouraged to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
